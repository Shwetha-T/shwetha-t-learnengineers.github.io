VTU RESULTS

Students check your results through the following steps

Step 1: Visit the official website of VTU - vtu.ac.in

Step 2: Go to the Examination tab and click on Results

Step 3: The result page will open. On this page there is a window that has CBCS and Non-CBCS option, select the relevant option and then click on the link to check the result.

Step 4: Now a new page will open. Enter your credentials(USN and Captche code)which is required to view your result

Step 5: Then click on the submit button

Step 6: Download and take a printout of your respective result for future references

Step 7:Check your respective University class Rank at vtu4u -Click Here
